Club History

Manchester United Football Club was founded in 1878 as Newton Heath. Based at Old Trafford, the club has grown into one of the biggest and most successful football teams in the world, with millions of fans globally.

Success & Trophies

Manchester United are the most successful club in English football history, winning 20 league titles, 12 FA Cups, and 3 UEFA Champions League trophies. Their dominance under Sir Alex Ferguson defined a golden era.

Club Philosophy

Known for attacking football, youth development, and fighting spirit, Manchester United represents pride, passion, and never giving up — values that define the club on and off the pitch.
